# Findings

## Spear Phishing

* The assessment demonstrated that targeted phishing messages can successfully persuade users to disclose sensitive information.
* Lack of user awareness and verification of sender identity increases the risk of credential theft.
* A successful attack may result in unauthorized access to user accounts and organizational resources.
* **OWASP Reference:** A07:2021 – Identification and Authentication Failures.
* **Risk Level:** High

## Clone Website

* A cloned website was able to closely resemble a legitimate login page, increasing the likelihood of users submitting sensitive credentials.
* Insufficient verification of website authenticity exposes users to phishing attacks and account compromise.
* Successful credential capture may lead to unauthorized access and further security incidents.
* **OWASP Reference:** A07:2021 – Identification and Authentication Failures.
* **Risk Level:** High
